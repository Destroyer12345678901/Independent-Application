How To Train Your Dog

SIT!

For this, your going to heavily depend on the treats you have.

Your dog will be very dangerous and not obedient to you unless trained.

Teach your dog how to sit. It will take a while. You might have to push the top part of the backside hard to make him or her sit down.

After it does so, give a treat to him or her and tell her Good Boy! or Good Girl! and then say Sit! He or she will be aware that it is doing the right thing and will thereby sit when asked to.

DOWN!

Teach it to lie down. When it lies down on it's bed or on the floor, say Good Boy! if male or Good Girl! if female, and give her a treat to make her know she is doing the right thing, and say Down as you do so.

When you train her to do more things, you now have a good, friendly dog.
